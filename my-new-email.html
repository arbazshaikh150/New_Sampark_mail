Chhattisgarh Young Scientist Award

Mr. Agniv Tapadar, a research scholar and PhD student in the Electrical Engineering Department, has been awarded the prestigious "Chhattisgarh Young Scientist Award -2023" in the division "Computer Science, IT, Electronics, and Instrumentation" as a recognition of his path-breaking research on "Portable Milk Adulteration Detector based on Fractional Order Colpitts Oscillator.".
