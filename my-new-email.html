Meraz'24 - Day 1

The annual techno-cultural fest, Meraz 2024, held at IIT Bhilai, commenced with great fervor on March 15 and continued for three days
Day 1 started with palpable energy, as the youth enthusiastically engaged in activities such as Robo-Kabaddi, Treasure Hunt, and Drone Flying. The campus buzzed with excitement as participants showcased their skills and competitive spirit.

 

Meraz'24 - Day 2

Day 2 of Meraz '24 was thrilling, offering various events, including Casino, Turbo Tide, and the much-anticipated Entrepreneurship Conclave. The day reached its pinnacle with a captivating performance by the renowned music band Qafila, leaving the audience spellbound..
 

.
 

Meraz'24 - Day 3

Day 3 of MERAZ wrapped up with adrenaline-pumping dare games and heartwarming moments at speed dating. The night ended with a stand-up performance by Vivek Samtani and a soul-stirring performance by singer Antara Mitra. .
 

Chhattisgarh Young Scientist Award

Mr. Agniv Tapadar, a dedicated research scholar and Ph.D. candidate in the Electrical Engineering Department, has been honored with the esteemed "Chhattisgarh Young Scientist Award - 2023." This recognition, bestowed upon him in the "Computer Science, IT, Electronics, and Instrumentation" division, acknowledges his groundbreaking research on developing a "Portable Milk Adulteration Detector based on Fractional Order Colpitts Oscillator." .
 

Women's Day

IIT Bhilai has successfully organized an engaging and informative event for Women's Day with our distinguished alumni, Miss Kumari Rani ( graduate of 2020 ), who currently works at Google. Her words of wisdom resonated deeply with the students, who had an excellent opportunity for one-on-one interaction with her..

Session By PAN IIT Alumni Chairman & Executive Committee Member

A special visit and expert talk featuring Mr. Debashish Bhattacharya, Chairman of PAN IIT Alumni, and Mr. Rajeev Gupta, Executive Committee Member of PAN IIT Alumni, concluded successfully. Their valuable insights and experiences from PAN IIT Alumni India have shed light on new pathways for our community. .
 

Student Mentorship Program

The Student Mentorship Program (SMP) conducted a felicitation ceremony on February 28, 2024, to acknowledge the contributions of student mentor coordinators, mentors, and volunteers toward the student orientation programs. .
 

India’s Techade: Chips for Viksit Bharat

The esteemed Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, addressed the youth of India through a video conference on the occasion of 'India's Techade: Chips for Viksit Bharat.' Students and faculty members of IIT Bhilai wholeheartedly participated in this significant event, demonstrating their keen interest and dedication to advancing technology and innovation in the nation.
