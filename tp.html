Dedication Of Permanent Campus

The Hon'ble Prime Minister Of India, Shri Narendra Modi Ji, dedicated the IIT Bhilai permanent campus to the nation. This a big step toward the Hon'ble PM's vision of making India the global Skill Capital.

Indo-German Workshop

German Workshop on the 'Integration of Sensors with AI Hardware' Organised at IIT Bhilai. The workshop was aimed at bringing together industry experts, researchers and professionals from both India and Germany to explore the potential, challenges and advancements in the field of green hydrogen production

Caldam 2024

The International Conference on Algorithms and Discrete Applied Mathematics (CALDAM), is intended to bring together researchers working in the areas of algorithms and applied discrete mathematics and provide a high-quality forum for the dissemination and discussion of research results in these broad areas. CALDAM has originated from the ongoing efforts for promoting research in Algorithms and Discrete Mathematics.
